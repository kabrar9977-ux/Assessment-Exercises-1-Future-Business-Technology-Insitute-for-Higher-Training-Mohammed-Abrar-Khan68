Yellow Label Black Tea

This is a classic black tea recipe!

Ingredients

  • put the tea bag in a teapot
  • 500ml boiling water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)

Method

  • First take a teapot and place the tea bag in it.
  • Next, pour the boiling water over the tea bag.
  • Let it steep for 3-5 minutes.
  • Strain the tea into a cup.
  • Add sugar if desired and stir until dissolved.
