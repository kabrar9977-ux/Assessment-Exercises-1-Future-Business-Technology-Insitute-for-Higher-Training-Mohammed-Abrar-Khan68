The Epic Story

The Final Chapter of the Infinity Saga

Follow the remaining Avengers as they attempt to undo the devastating "Snap" and restore balance to the universe in this breathtaking conclusion.

Complete Plot Summary

Avengers: Endgame (2019) follows the surviving members of the Avengers as they attempt to undo the "Snap," an event from Infinity War where Thanos used the Infinity Stones to erase half of all life in the universe.

Act I: The Aftermath and a New Hope

Twenty-three days after the Snap, Carol Danvers rescues Tony Stark and Nebula from space. The team tracks Thanos to "The Garden," hoping to use the Stones to reverse his actions, only to find he has destroyed them to prevent their further use. Out of rage, Thor beheads Thanos, and the team is left defeated.

Five years later, in 2023, Scott Lang (Ant-Man) escapes the Quantum Realm and realizes that while five years passed on Earth, only five hours passed for him. He proposes a "Time Heist" to retrieve the Infinity Stones from the past.

Act II: The Time Heist

The Avengers split into teams and travel to different points in MCU history:

2012 New York

Bruce Banner (now "Smart Hulk") retrieves the Time Stone from the Ancient One, while Steve Rogers secures the Mind Stone. Tony Stark and Scott Lang fail to get the Space Stone after 2012 Loki escapes with it.

1970 New Jersey

To recover the Space Stone, Stark and Rogers travel further back to a S.H.I.E.L.D. base, where they also obtain more Pym Particles.

2013 Asgard

Thor and Rocket retrieve the Reality Stone from Jane Foster.

2014 Morag and Vormir

Nebula and James Rhodes take the Power Stone, but 2014-Thanos discovers their plan through Nebula's shared memory files. On Vormir, Natasha Romanoff (Black Widow) sacrifices her life so Clint Barton (Hawkeye) can obtain the Soul Stone.

Act III: The Final Stand

Back in the present, Hulk uses a new nano-gauntlet to snap everyone back to life. However, the 2014 version of Nebula (impersonating her future self) brings 2014-Thanos and his army to 2023.

Thanos destroys the Avengers Compound, leading to a massive battle where the resurrected heroes arrive via portals created by Doctor Strange and other sorcerers. In the climax, Tony Stark steals the Infinity Stones from Thanos and snaps his own fingers to disintegrate Thanos's army, sacrificing his life in the process.

Act IV: Conclusion

Following the battle, a funeral is held for Tony Stark. Thor appoints Valkyrie to lead New Asgard and joins the Guardians of the Galaxy. An aged Steve Rogers passes his shield to Sam Wilson after returning the stones to their proper timelines.
