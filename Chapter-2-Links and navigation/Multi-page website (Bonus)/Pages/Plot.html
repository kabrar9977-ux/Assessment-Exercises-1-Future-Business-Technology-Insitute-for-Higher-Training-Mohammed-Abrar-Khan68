The Plot

Avengers Endgame is about the superheroes trying to stop the bad guy Thanos.

What happens

After Thanos snaps his fingers and half the people disappear, the Avengers try to find a way to bring them back.

They find a way to go back in time and get special stones that will help them.

In the end, Iron Man uses the stones and stops Thanos, but he dies.

The End

It is a very sad ending but the universe is saved.
