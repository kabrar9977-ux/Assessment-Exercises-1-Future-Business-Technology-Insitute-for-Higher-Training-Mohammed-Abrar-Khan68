Legendary Heroes

The Characters Who Defined an Era

From genius billionaires to super soldiers, these extraordinary individuals came together to face their greatest challenge and save the universe from certain destruction.

The Avengers Roster

🦾

Iron Man

Tony Stark

The genius billionaire philanthropist whose arc reactor technology and brilliant mind make him the heart of the Avengers. His final sacrifice saves all of reality.

Genius Intellect Advanced Armor Leadership
🛡️

Captain America

Steve Rogers

The First Avenger and super soldier who leads with honor and courage. Worthy of wielding Mjolnir, he proves that true power comes from character.

Super Strength Enhanced Agility Tactical Genius
🔨

Thor

Thor Odinson

The God of Thunder and Asgardian prince who wields the mighty Stormbreaker. His journey from royal arrogance to humble hero defines his character arc.

Lightning Control Godlike Strength Immortality
⚡

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff

The master spy and assassin who becomes one of Earth's mightiest heroes. Her sacrifice on Vormir proves that family comes in all forms.

Expert Combat Espionage Strategic Mind
🤖

Hulk

Bruce Banner

The brilliant scientist who achieves perfect balance with his gamma-powered alter ego. As Smart Hulk, he brings hope back to the universe.

Super Strength Scientific Genius Durability
🏹

Hawkeye

Clint Barton

The master archer and family man who becomes Ronin after losing his family to the Snap. His journey represents the human cost of Thanos' actions.

Perfect Aim Martial Arts Tactical Skills
🐜

Ant-Man

Scott Lang

The quantum hero whose discovery of time travel gives the Avengers their only hope. His humor and heart keep the team grounded.

Size Manipulation Quantum Access Tech Expertise
💫

Captain Marvel

Carol Danvers

The cosmic powerhouse whose arrival turns the tide of battle. Her immense strength and determination make her one of the universe's greatest defenders.

Cosmic Power Flight Energy Absorption
