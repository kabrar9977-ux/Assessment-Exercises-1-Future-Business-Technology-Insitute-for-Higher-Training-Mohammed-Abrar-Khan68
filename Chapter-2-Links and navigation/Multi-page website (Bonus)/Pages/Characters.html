The Characters

Here are the main superheroes in Avengers Endgame.

Iron Man

Iron Man is a smart guy with a suit. He fights bad guys.

Captain America

Captain America is very strong and brave. He is a good leader.

Black Widow

Black Widow is a spy and fighter. She is very fast.

Thor

Thor is a god from another world. He has a hammer.
